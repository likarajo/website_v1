About Me

Rajarshi Chattopadhyay is a graduate student at the University of Texas at Dallas, majoring in Computer Science.
He is experienced in web development and database design, and has in-depth knowledge of shipping industry.

He is a former System Engineer at IBM - Client Innovation Centre, India, where he served as Application Developer in the Distribution sector.
He interned at Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd. and SIEMENS Metal technologies, where he worked on Computer Networking and Automation respectively. He also worked part-time at Hi-Q Solutions on embedded systems.

Rajarshi received his Bachelor of Technology degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT), Kolkata, India.

His cross-disciplinary research interests lie in areas of Big Data management and analysis, and Applied Machine Learning.
